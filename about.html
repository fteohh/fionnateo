A bit about me

Hello! I'm Fionna, my pronouns are she/they, and I'm an aspiring digital product designer who seeks to foster empathy and positive change through user experience and visual design. I grew up in Fort Smith, AR, and currently live in Evanston, IL, as a senior at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Japanese.

I grew up as an art kid and always strive to combine my passion for visual art with creative problem-solving. My interest in issues such as accessibility, social justice, and sustainability also guide my approach to center inclusivity and functionality in design.

In my free time, I can be found on a long walk, trying new restuarants, filling pages in my sketchbook, and treasure hunting at the thrift store.
